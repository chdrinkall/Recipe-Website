Lasagna Recipe

This is how you make lasagna

First you have to buy all of the ingredients to make this. They include many items so make sure you are up for the task.

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Little bit of love

This is how you make this dish

  1. Put the noodles in a pot
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Add noodles to a pan
  4. Add meat on top of the noodles
  5. Add everything else and cook the dish
  6. Enjoy the meal
